Welcome to MyCouncilMobile.

MyCouncilMobile is a project initiated by Northampton Borough Council

The aim of the MyCouncilMobile project is to create a single Open Source mobile app capable of delivering any Local Authority functionality. It has been designed from the outset to be easily repurposed by other Local Authorities for their use.

v1.0 Has already been released and can be found on the app store here

Current Functionality

  • Report-It : Environmental Reporting direct to a CRM : defaults to Kana's Lagan
  • Find-It : Get your refuse collection details and set a reminder on the phone.
  • Contact-Us : An categorised eForm for intelligent routing and analysis of contacts.
  • Social Feed : A timeline of news, articles, comments and updates for your area.
